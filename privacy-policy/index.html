Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we handle your data at Vocalsplit.app.

1. Our Core Privacy Principle

The most important thing to know about Vocalsplit.app is that your audio files NEVER leave your computer. Unlike traditional online audio tools, our AI processing happens entirely within your web browser.

  • No audio data is uploaded to our servers.
  • No audio data is stored, analyzed, or shared by us.
  • Processing is 100% local (client-side).

2. Information We Collect

Since we do not process your audio on our servers, we only collect minimal technical information to maintain and improve the website:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information such as browser type, time spent on the site, and which tools are used most frequently.
  • Technical Logs: Standard web server logs may include your IP address for security and anti-fraud purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience and support our free service. This includes:

  • Essential cookies for site functionality.
  • Preference cookies (e.g., remembering your theme settings).
  • Third-party cookies from partners like Ezoic or Google for analytics and ads.

You can manage these at any time via our Cookie Settings page.

4. Data Security

Because your audio files remain on your local device, there is no risk of your private media being intercepted or leaked from our servers. We implement standard security measures (SSL/HTTPS) to protect the delivery of the website code to your browser.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external platforms.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.

Summary: We provide a professional AI tool that respects your data. You keep your files; we only provide the technology to process them privately in your browser.
