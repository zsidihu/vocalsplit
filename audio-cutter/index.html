How to Cut and Trim Audio Online?

Trimming your audio files is simple and fast. With VocalSplit.app, you can cut any MP3, WAV, or OGG file directly in your browser without waiting for uploads or downloads to a server.

How it works in 3 simple steps

  1. Upload: Select your audio file. Since there is no server upload, it opens instantly regardless of file size.
  2. Adjust: Use the intuitive timeline and sliders to select the exact part of the song you want to edit.
  3. Process: Choose to either keep the selection (Trim) or remove it (Cut). Your new file is ready for download immediately.

Why choose our Browser-Based Audio Cutter?

Traditional online tools require you to upload your files to their servers, which poses privacy risks and wastes time. VocalSplit.app uses your browser's local processing power. Your data never leaves your computer, making it the fastest and most secure way to edit private recordings.

Two powerful editing modes

Unlike basic tools, our editor offers two distinct ways to handle your audio:

  • Keep Selection (Trim): Perfect for extracting a specific highlight, creating ringtones, or shortening a track.
  • Remove Selection (Cut & Join): Ideal for deleting unwanted ads from podcasts, removing silence, or cutting out mistakes while automatically joining the remaining parts.
