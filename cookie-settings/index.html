Cookie Settings

Manage how we use cookies to provide a secure and efficient experience on Vocalsplit.app.

At Vocalsplit.app, we prioritize your privacy. While our AI stem splitting happens 100% locally in your browser and your audio files are never uploaded to any server, we use cookies to ensure the website functions correctly and to improve our free tools.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are essential for remembering your preferences and helping us understand how you interact with our platform.

How We Use Cookies

Our cookies are categorized into the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are strictly necessary for the website to function. They handle basic features like page navigation and secure access.
  • Performance & Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA) to collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site. This helps us optimize the processing speed and fix layout issues.
  • User Experience (UX): These cookies remember your settings, such as dark/light mode preferences, to provide a consistent experience.
  • Advertising & Third-Party Scripts: To keep our tools free, we may use partners like Ezoic. These providers use cookies to show relevant ads and prevent fraud.

Managing Your Preferences

You have the right to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie settings through their options menu. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how the website performs for you.

Privacy Reminder: Because Vocalsplit.app operates entirely client-side, your audio data is never shared with any third-party cookie providers.

How to Opt-Out?

If you wish to block Google Analytics specifically, you can install the GA Opt-out Browser Add-on. For other cookies, please check your browser's "Privacy and Security" settings.
