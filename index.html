Your privacy is our priority. Unlike other AI splitters, we do not upload your audio files to any server. All the heavy AI computation happens locally on your device. This means zero waiting for uploads, no data collection, and instant results.

How to Split Vocals and Music?

  1. Upload: Select your song (MP3, WAV, or OGG). The AI model loads directly into your browser memory.
  2. Process: Click the "Process" button. Our multi-threaded engine will start separating the vocals from the music in real-time.
  3. Download: Once finished, you can listen to and download the individual stems (Vocals and Backing Track) separately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a file size limit?

While we recommend files between 50MB and 100MB for optimal performance, our engine can process larger files depending on your system's RAM. Since everything happens locally in your browser, the only limit is your hardware's capability.

How does VocalSplit compare to cloud-based tools?

Most online tools (like LALAL.ai) require server uploads and often have queues or paywalls. We process audio multi-threaded on your local machine, offering 100% privacy and no waiting times for free.

What file formats are supported?

We support all major formats including MP3, WAV, and OGG. For the best AI separation results, we recommend using high-quality WAV files when possible.
